Tanja Miju

Event Details

Get ready for a night of unrelenting energy as Black Room, Corruption and Clandestine come together to deliver Miami's FIRST underground rave of 2025 presenting German DJ Tanja Miju.

This is no ordinary night. It's a journey into the deep, dark pulse of underground techno, where the kicks never stop, and the bass reverberates through your soul.

Tanja Miju will take you on a sonic adventure, blending driving rhythms and dark melodies that will have the dance floor moving like never before.

Joining her will be a stellar lineup of your favorite locals, Dadrev B2B Instinct, Domnrob, and Quetamine.

With a secret location, cutting-edge production, and a crowd that thrives on the thrill of the underground, this is one rave you do not want to miss.

Join us January 10th for this special event.
